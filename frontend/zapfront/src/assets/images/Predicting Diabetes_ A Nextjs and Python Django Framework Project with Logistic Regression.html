Introduction

Welcome everyone to this presentation on our exciting new project that aims to help people understand and prevent diabetes and related diseases. Our project is built using some of the most cutting-edge technologies available today, including Nextjs, Python Django Rest Framework, and the logistic regression project.

Our goal with this project is to provide a user-friendly platform that allows people to easily calculate their BMI, predict their likelihood of developing diabetes and related diseases, and access important information and resources to help them maintain a healthy lifestyle. We believe that this project has the potential to make a real difference in people's lives, and we're excited to share it with you today.

4

Website Overview

Welcome to our website, where we aim to provide users with valuable information and tools related to diabetes prevention and management. Our website has three main tabs: Home, BMI, and Prediction.

The Home tab provides general information about diabetes and its causes, as well as tips for prevention and management. The BMI tab allows users to calculate their body mass index (BMI) and provides information on maintaining a healthy weight. Finally, the Prediction tab uses a logistic regression model to predict a user's risk of developing diabetes based on various inputs such as age, gender, and medical history.

5

Prediction Tab

The Prediction tab is the heart of our website, where users can input their personal information and receive a calculated diabetes prediction percentage. The form takes into account age, gender, HbA1c level, blood glucose level, and questions about smoking, hypertension, and heart disease, all of which contribute to the overall prediction.

The gauge meter on the page shows the calculated diabetes prediction in percentage, with corresponding probable diseases and precautions. This helps users understand what actions they need to take to prevent or manage diabetes, as well as providing valuable information on related diseases that may be a concern. Our website's prediction tool is an essential resource for anyone looking to take control of their health.

6

BMI Tab

The BMI tab is an essential tool on our website that helps users understand their body mass index and provides precautions to maintain a healthy BMI. Body mass index, or BMI, is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It is a useful tool for determining whether an individual's weight is healthy or not. The height vs weight table on the BMI tab shows users where they fall on the BMI scale and provides a visual representation of what a healthy weight range looks like.

Our BMI calculator takes user inputs of height and weight and calculates their BMI for them. We also provide information on what a healthy BMI range is and what precautions users can take to achieve and maintain a healthy BMI. Maintaining a healthy BMI is important because it can reduce the risk of many chronic diseases, including diabetes, heart disease, and stroke.

7

Python Logistic Regression

The Python logistic regression project is a key component of our website's functionality. Its purpose is to predict the likelihood of diabetes and related diseases based on user inputs, including age, gender, HbA1c level, blood glucose level, smoking status, hypertension, and heart disease.

The logistic regression algorithm used in the project is trained on a large dataset of medical records and uses statistical analysis to identify patterns and relationships between risk factors and diabetes. By inputting user data into the algorithm, the project generates a prediction of the user's likelihood of developing diabetes and provides information on related diseases and precautions to take.

8

Conclusion

In conclusion, our website is a valuable resource for anyone looking to understand and prevent diabetes and related diseases. Through the use of Nextjs, Python Django Rest Framework, and the logistic regression project, we have created a platform that provides users with accurate predictions and helpful information.

We hope that you take advantage of our website's resources and tools, including the BMI calculator, prediction tab, and informative articles. By understanding your risk factors and taking proactive steps towards prevention, you can improve your health and wellbeing. Thank you for joining us on this journey towards a healthier future.
